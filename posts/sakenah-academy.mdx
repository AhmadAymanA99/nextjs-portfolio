## Introduction:

Sakenah Academy is a training & educational platform in mental health/psychology. The platform provides comprehensive educational resources and training programs for professionals and individuals interested in mental health and psychology.

<Callout type="info">
  This project was developed as part of the Softera team's healthcare education portfolio.
</Callout>

### My Contribution:

As part of the Softera team, I contributed to the development and support of Sakenah Academy, working on:
• Platform development and maintenance
• Feature implementation for educational content delivery
• User experience enhancements
• Integration with backend services
